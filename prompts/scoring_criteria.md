# Scoring Criteria

Score each opportunity from 1 to 5.

5 = Pitch now
- Timely, clearly Canadian, and already newsworthy.
- Strong connection to Charitable Impact, donor-advised funds, donors, charities, or giving infrastructure.
- John can add a distinct, credible perspective.
- A journalist could use a quote or interview today.

4 = Prepare commentary
- Relevant and timely, but may need one more data point, local example, or stronger hook.
- Good fit for a drafted quote, op-ed outline, or targeted outreach.

3 = Monitor
- Potentially useful trend or early signal.
- Connection exists, but the public conversation is not urgent enough yet.

2 = Keep for content
- Useful for owned content, newsletters, social posts, or background research.
- Weak earned-media hook.

1 = Ignore
- Off lane, stale, too speculative, not Canadian enough, or too weakly connected.
